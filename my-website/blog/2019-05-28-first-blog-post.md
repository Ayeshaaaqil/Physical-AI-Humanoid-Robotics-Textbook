## Vision-Language-Action Pipeline

The VLA pipeline is the backbone of Physical AI:

1. Speech captured via microphones  
2. Whisper converts voice to commands  
3. GPT plans task execution  
4. ROS Actions move actuators  
5. Sensors provide feedback loops

This pipeline enables everyday commands like:

> _"Navigate to the desk and bring me the object."_
